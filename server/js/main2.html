סרטוטי מעגלים חשמליים

The camera robot consists of several key components that enable its functionality. These include:

  1. Camera module: The camera module captures images and records videos.
  2. Robot arm: The robot arm allows the camera to move and rotate in different directions.
  3. Processing unit: The processing unit handles the camera's operations and image processing tasks.
  4. Sensors: Sensors provide feedback to the camera robot, allowing it to navigate and detect obstacles.
